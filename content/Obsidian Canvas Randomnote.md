**Obsidian Canvas Randomnote** is a plugin for [Obsidian]([https://obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md/)).

It allows you to add a grid of random notes to the Obsidian canvas based on the contents of your active search. I personally use it for reviewing a random selection of things for inspiration.
